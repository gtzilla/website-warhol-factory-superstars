Warhol Factory SuperStars

Collectible Caricatures

An elegant, collectible collection of Caricatures. Give them as gifts or decorate your office! There are many ways to enjoy these iconic celebrities.

Every print is numbered and signed by the Artist, Danielle Ancrile. Each image is drawn by hand, then scanned into a computer. Finally, it's enhanced with digital tools to create the quirky, energetic caricatures that bring these iconic stars to life.

Each image is custom printed onto Metallic, Archival, paper, bringing a smooth finish to each image.
