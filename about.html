About Danielle

Danielle Ancrile is a New York based graphic designer and artist. Danielle was born and raised in Florida. In 2012, she decided to pursue her dreams in New York. In 2016, Danielle was accepted to the New School for graphic design where she is actively pursuing an associates degree. Danielle also holds a Certificate of Graphic Design from NYU. Before focusing on art full-time, Danielle worked in both the finance and insurance industries. Danielle lives in Brooklyn with her fianc√©, Gregory and their two cats.
